## Title

MEKAI Longitudinal Vehicle Starter Lite

## Summary

Free Simulink baseline for longitudinal vehicle dynamics and speed-control prototyping.

## Description

MEKAI Longitudinal Vehicle Starter Lite is a free community edition built for engineers, consultants, students, and researchers who want a clean MATLAB / Simulink baseline for longitudinal vehicle simulation.

The pack includes a simple starter model, setup script, build script, and preview output so users can inspect the structure and evaluate the baseline quickly.

Best use cases:

- longitudinal dynamics exploration
- speed-control studies
- teaching and internal demos
- early-stage controls prototyping

This submission is positioned as a free baseline, not as a production-ready vehicle model.
